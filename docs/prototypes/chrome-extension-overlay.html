Senior Product Manager — Growth
Spotify · Amsterdam, Netherlands · Hybrid
Posted 2 days ago 201–500 applicants €90,000–€115,000/yr

About the role

We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to join our Growth team in Amsterdam. You will be responsible for driving user acquisition and activation across our freemium funnel, working closely with engineering, design, data science, and marketing.

What you'll do:

Define and execute the product strategy for growth initiatives. Lead cross-functional squads to deliver measurable improvements to our activation and monetisation funnels. Own the roadmap from ideation through to launch and iteration. Analyse user behaviour data to identify growth opportunities and surface insights.

What we're looking for:

5+ years of product management experience, with at least 2 years in growth or monetisation. Strong data analysis skills — comfortable with SQL, Amplitude, and experimentation frameworks. Experience working in a B2C subscription model. Excellent written and verbal communication skills. Fluent in English; Dutch or Swedish is a plus.

What we offer:

Competitive salary (€90k–€115k depending on experience). 30 days paid leave. Learning budget €2,000/year. Relocation support for candidates outside the Netherlands.
