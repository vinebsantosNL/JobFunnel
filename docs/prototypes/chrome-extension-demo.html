About the role

We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to join our Growth team in Amsterdam. You will be responsible for driving user acquisition and activation across our freemium funnel, working closely with engineering, design, data science, and marketing.

What you'll do:

  • Define and execute the product strategy for growth initiatives across Spotify Free and Premium
  • Lead cross-functional squads to deliver measurable improvements to our activation and monetisation funnels
  • Own the roadmap from ideation through to launch, iteration, and scaling
  • Analyse user behaviour using SQL, Amplitude, and internal data tools to surface growth opportunities
  • Partner with marketing on A/B testing programmes and growth experiments
  • Communicate product direction and results to senior leadership

What we're looking for:

  • 5+ years of product management experience, with at least 2 years in growth or monetisation
  • Strong data analysis skills — comfortable with SQL, experimentation frameworks, and statistical thinking
  • Experience working in a high-growth B2C subscription model
  • Track record of shipping products that measurably improved activation or conversion
  • Excellent English communication; Dutch or Swedish is a bonus

What we offer:

  • Competitive salary: €90k–€115k depending on experience
  • 30 days paid annual leave
  • Annual learning & development budget: €2,000
  • Relocation support for candidates outside the Netherlands
  • Equity participation in Spotify's long-term incentive plan
