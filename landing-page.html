FAQ

Questions worth asking
before you sign up.

Is this just another job tracker?

Most job trackers give you a Kanban board and a count of how many applications you've sent. That's useful for staying organised. It's not a funnel.

Job Funnel tracks stage-by-stage conversion rates, time in stage, and lets you A/B test which CV version gets more screenings. If you've outgrown a tracker and want data that actually drives decisions, that's the difference.

What kind of insights does it actually give me?

Concrete ones. Instead of "you have 47 open applications", you see:

Applied → Screening: 9% (bench: 18%) — likely CV or targeting issue
Screening → Interviewing: 55% (bench: 50%) — performing above average
Interviewing → Offer: 22% (bench: 28%) — small gap, worth reviewing

You know exactly which stage to fix. Not a guess — a diagnosis.

I only have 5–8 applications right now. Is it too early?

The free tier handles that comfortably — 5 active applications, full pipeline view, no credit card.

The honest answer: conversion analytics get meaningful at 10–15 applications. But the story bank and CV versioning are useful from day one, even with 3 applications. Starting early means your first 20 applications are producing signal, not just noise.

How long until I actually see useful conversion data?

For funnel rates: 10–15 applications across at least 3–4 stages gives you a first read. Most active searchers hit that in 2–3 weeks.

For CV A/B testing: you need enough applications on each version to trust the numbers — usually 10+ per version. We flag low-confidence results with a "not enough data" badge so you don't optimise on noise.

I'm based in the EU — what about GDPR and my data?

Job Funnel is built in Europe, for Europe. GDPR compliance is built into the data model — not bolted on afterward. Your job search data is yours, it's not sold, and it's not used to train AI models. You can export or delete everything at any time.

We'll publish a full data practices page before launch. If you have specific questions, email us directly at privacy@jobfunnel.io.

What happens when I find a job? Do I keep paying?

Cancel the moment you land the role — no penalty, no retention dark patterns.

Most users complete a search in 4–6 months. When you start your next search in 2–3 years, your story bank and pipeline history will still be there. About half of our users come back when they search again — which is why we'd rather you cancel cleanly and come back than feel trapped in a subscription you're not using.
